2."The Case of the Kindaichi Boy"

Introduction

""The Case of the Kindaichi Teenager" is a TV animation of the same name adapted from the manga of the same name "The Story of the Kindaichi Boy", which was broadcast on April 7, 1997. Produced by Suwa Michihiko, produced by Toei Animation (renamed Toei Animation in 1998). From April 7, 1997 to September 11, 2000, it premiered on Yomiuri TV and Nippon Television Network (NNS) at 7:00 p.m. every Monday in Japan, with a total of 148 episodes, one SP episode, and two theatrical episodes; Two additional episodes of SP and two episodes of OVA were produced. In 2014, "Kindaichi Juvenile Case Book R" was broadcast, and in 2015, an episode of SP was produced again; "Kindaichi Juvenile Case Book R" was broadcast in the form of half a year..
